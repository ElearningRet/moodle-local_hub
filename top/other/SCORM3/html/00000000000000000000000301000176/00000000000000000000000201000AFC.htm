Summary
In this lesson you learned about how e-Learning has changed as networking changed over the last several decades. This includes:
 
  • Mainframe networks of the 1960s and 1970s

  • LAN and WAN networks of the 1970s and 1980s

  • Internetworks and the Internet in the 1980s through today
